The Issue

Over 6.1 million families accessed emergency food supplies from their local food pantry last year. These pantries desperately need new food donations to feed hungry families this holiday season.

What You Can Do

Collect 25 cans of food and drop them off at your local food pantry. Your donations will help feed a hungry family in your community.

Scholarship

Collect and donate at least 25 food items and you’ll get one entry for the $5,000 scholarship. For every 25 items you collect after that, you’ll be entered into the drawing again.
