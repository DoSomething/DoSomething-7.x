The Issue

Over 6.1 million families accessed food pantries last year during a crisis, and food pantries are in constant need of new supplies, especially after a disaster like Hurricane Sandy.

What You Can Do

Run a food drive to stock your local food pantry. Your donations will help families currently in crisis, and prepare against future natural disasters.

Scholarship

Collect and donate at least 25 food items and you’ll get one entry for the $5,000 scholarship. For every 25 items you collect after that, you’ll be entered into the drawing again.
