What is Pantry Prep?

Every county in the United States is affected by hunger. We asked young people across the United States to stock their local food pantry to help hungry families in their communities, and participants collected 567,068 pounds of food (whoa!).

How can canned and dry food help?

Donating non-perishable items such as canned and dry goods makes it easier for food pantries to distribute food to families quickly with little waste in a crisis.
