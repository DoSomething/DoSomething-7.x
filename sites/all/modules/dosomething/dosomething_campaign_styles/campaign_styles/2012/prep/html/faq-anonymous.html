What is Pantry Prep?

We received thousands of requests from individuals who need help after Hurricane Sandy. We're asking young people across the United States to help by running a food drive and donating their items to their local food pantry. This is especially important for people affected by the storm, but even if you weren't affected, disaster preparedness is one biggest ways a community can arm itself when disaster strikes.

How can canned and dry food help?

Food banks play a critical role when a disaster strikes. They provide food to people who may lose access to food, power, or their homes. Donating non-perishable items such as canned and dry goods makes it easier for food banks to distribute food to families when a disaster strikes with little waste.

What type of food should I collect?

Food banks most frequently requested items include:

  • canned meats (i.e., tuna, chicken, salmon)
  • canned and boxed meals (i.e., soup, chili, stew, macaroni and cheese)
  • peanut butter and other nut butters
  • canned or dried beans and peas (i.e., black, pinto, lentils)
  • pasta, rice, cereal
  • canned fruits and vegetables
  • 100 percent fruit or vegetable juice (canned, plastic or boxed)
  • cooking oil
  • shelf-stable milk
  • consider donating healthy, low-fat and low-sugar products

What else can I do to help people affected by Sandy?

If you have the below supplies available please take them to your local shelters as well:

  • Bottled water
  • Iodine tablets
  • Clorox
  • Cleaning soap
  • Clothes detergent
  • Towels
  • Blankets
  • Bed sheets
  • Air mattresses (w/pumps, if possible)
  • Over the counter medicine (advil, aspirin, etc.)
  • Hygiene products (bars of soap, shampoo)
  • Rubbing alcohol
  • Hydrogen peroxide
  • Clothes (in particular: sweaters/sweat shirts, sweats, jeans, socks, underwear) = mainly for adults: men & women
  • Bood: mostly type O, but also A & B (not AB, but if available)
  • Gloves
  • Head gear for warmth
  • Baby wipes
  • Hand sanitizer
  • Sanitizing wipes
  • Tooth brushes
  • Tooth paste
  • Mouthwash
  • Deodorant (unisex, if possible)
