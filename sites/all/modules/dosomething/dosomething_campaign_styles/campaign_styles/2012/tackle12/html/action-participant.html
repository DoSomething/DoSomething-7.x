How to Run Your Drive

Do This Now:

Recruit. You can’t run your drive alone. Invite friends to attend or help with your drive.

Save the date. Set a time, date, and public location for your drive. Make sure to ask for permission.

Two Weeks Out:

Get loud. Use Facebook and Twitter to publicize your drive by sharing these stats on your friends Facebook walls.

Make friends. Contact your local newspaper to see if they’ll do a story on your drive. See how local businesses can support your efforts.

Almost There:

Show up early on the day of your drive to brief your team on their roles and to organize. Take lots of photos and document how much food you’ve collected!

Bring it home:

Use our food bank locator to find your local food bank to drop off your food. Click here to upload pics and tell us all about your drive!
