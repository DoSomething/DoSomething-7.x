What is 50 Cans?

50 Cans is the largest aluminum can drive run by young people. You’ll be making a huge difference by collecting and recycling aluminum, and at the same time, you can become a drive guru by using our Pocket Guide, which teaches you the ropes all through text messages.

How do I run a drive?

Step one is signing up for the campaign. By entering your email address or phone number, you choose whether you want to get advice on running a drive through email, texts or both.

How do I win a scholarship?

Here’s the breakdown: the scholarship winner will be chosen from a lottery. You get one ticket into the lottery by signing up and spreading the word to 6 of your friends and additional ticket for each 50 cans you collect. How will we know how many cans you’ve collected? Go to our Tell Us About It section and fill us in. In order to win, we’ll need you to submit a picture of all the cans you collected.
