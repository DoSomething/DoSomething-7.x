how to run your drive

DO THIS NOW!

  • A Be The Match recruiter will be in touch to help with your drive logistics. Once you have a date, location, and time, edit the info above for everyone who signs up for your drive.
  • Invite your friends to attend or help you plan your drive.
  • Secure a public location. Make sure to ask permission. (Why not ask them to help advertise your drive.)

TWO WEEKS OUT

  • Start advertising your drive by sharing some of these stats on your friends Facebook wall.
  • Reach out to your college paper, and a local paper to see if they'll do a story on your drive.
  • Continue to advertise - print fliers, get on your school radio, send out emails!

ALMOST THERE

  • On the day of your drive, show up 1 hour before your drive to have a status and training meeting with your recruiter. You and your volunteers will recruit, hand out forms and help with cheek swabbing.
  • Take pictures of all the people swabbing their cheeks! Uploading to social? Use #GiveASpit

WRAP IT UP!

  • upload your pics and tell us how many cheeks you swabbed!
