Quick description:

Ever year, 3 to 4 million animals in shelters are euthanized simply because they don't get adopted. One reason they don't get adopted? Bad pictures.

Using the Pics For Pets mobile app for iOS and Android, you can share or even take pictures of shelter animals in need, increasing their chances of being adopted.

    Feature Highlights:

  • Hundreds of shelter animals you can share via Facebook to help get adopted
  • Instructions on how to become a Fur-tographer and save a shelter animal's life yourself
  • Easy shelter locator
  • Use your camera to take pictures and upload to the app right from the shelter
  • Educational walk through to learn how to use the app
  • Prizing both for humans (a $10,000 scholarship) and the shelter animals (shelters will receive gift cards for toys, food, and bedding if the animal hits a certain level of shares).
