Do the supplies have to be new?

Yes, ideally all of the supplies should be unused. Having something fresh and new can be really powerful when what is surrounding you is all dirty and broken.

Do I have to send all of those supplies?

Nope! You can send one or all of the things on the list above.

Do I have to send a note with my supplies?

Only send a note if you want to. Sending a quick message saying “We’re thinking about you.” or “You’re in my thoughts.” can be therapeutic to give and receive, so we wanted to offer the option. If you’d rather send a message through your phone, text HOPE to 38383.
