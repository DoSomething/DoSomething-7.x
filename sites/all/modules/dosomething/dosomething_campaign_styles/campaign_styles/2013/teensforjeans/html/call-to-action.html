How It Works:

The Issue

About 1 in 3 people experiencing homelessness is under the age of eighteen. One of the items homeless youth request most is a pair of jeans.

Collect & Drop Off

Run a drive to collect as many jeans as you can! Drop off the jeans at your local Aéropostale from January 20th to February 16th. Don't forget to snap pics of your drive and drop-off!

Tell Us

To enter your school to win the grand prize — and for another chance to win the $4,000 scholarship — tell us how many pairs of jeans you and your school collected.
