Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I do this?

When people enter a homeless shelter, they often don’t have more than the clothes they are wearing. Jeans are often requested because they provide a sense of normalcy, can be worn for several days before they need to washed, and are comfortable during every season.

What happens after I drop off my jeans at my local Aéropostale store?

Each Aéropostale and P.S. from Aéropostale store is paired with at least one homeless shelter in your community. In total, over 1,000 homeless shelters across the U.S., Canada, and Puerto Rico are participating in Teens for Jeans 2014.

How do I get involved?

Sign up to run a jeans drive at your school. Then spread the word, collect gently worn jeans, and visit your local Aéropostale or P.S. from Aéropostale store to drop off what you collect. For each pair you drop off, you will receive a coupon for 25% off a new pair of jeans!

How do I get a coupon for 25% off a new pair of jeans?

Bring your jeans into your local Aéropostale or P.S. from Aéropostale store -- you'll get one coupon for each pair of jeans you donate.

If I donate more than one pair of jeans, do I get more than one coupon?

Yes! When you donate your jeans at Aéropostale or P.S. from Aéropostale, you'll receive one 25% coupon for each pair of jeans you donate.

Can I run my drive at my place of worship or other community center?

Yes! It will function just like a school drive. Just be sure to drop off the jeans you collect at your local Aéropostale store!

What is the banner for?

If you would like to run a jeans drive, hang the banner above your drive box to spread the word!

Can I donate jeans by myself?

Yes! Just bring them to your local Aéropostale store from January 20th to February 16th, 2014.

When can I drop jeans off?

You can drop off your jeans at your local Aéropostale store from January 20th to February 16th, 2014.

What sizes of jeans are you looking for?

Homeless shelters often have a greater need for large sizes, but we will accept jeans of all sizes for this campaign. All of the jeans should be in good condition.

Are Aéropostale and P.S. From Aéropostale stores accepting other clothes besides jeans?

For Teens for Jeans, we are just collecting jeans. If you have extra hats, shirts, etc., please donate them directly to your local homeless shelter or charity!

Does every Aéropostale and P.S. From Aéropostale store participate in this campaign?

Yes. Every Aéropostale and P.S. from Aéropostale store will be accepting jean donations.

Does it matter what brand of jeans I donate?

Nope. We want to collect as many jeans as possible! All we ask is that they are in good condition, so please only donate jeans that can be worn.

If I'm a shelter that wants to be involved as a jeans beneficiary, what do I do?

Please send an email to teensforjeans@dosomething.org and we will get back to you about participating in next year's campaign!

How do I win the $4,000 scholarship?

The scholarship winner will be picked randomly. To enter the lottery, register your school. To be entered again, tell us about your drive by filling out this form.

How do the school prizes work?

The school that collects the most jeans will win the grand prize: a $10,000 grant, Aéropostale t-shirts, and a party! The top collecting elementary school, middle school, high school, and Canadian school will each win $5K grants.

I didn't sign up with my school, but now I want to. Can I do that?

Yes! Just go , put in your school information, and push the "submit" button.

I still have more questions!

We’re here to help! Email Nami at teensforjeans@dosomething.org with your question and she’ll get back to you!
