Frequently Asked Questions

Where did my donated jeans go?

We match each Aéropostale store to the nearest homeless shelter or charity that serves youth and would like to participate. The store ships all the jeans dropped off to their charity partner. Usually, the organization is within 30 miles of the store.

Can I get involved now?
