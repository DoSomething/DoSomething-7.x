Why Should I do this?

Puppy mills are a dirty secret in the pet store world. Virtually all pets sold in pet stores or online are from puppy mills and buying from them supports these terrible practices. If you love puppies, the best way to fight for them is encourage your friends not to shop at pet stores that sell pets. The Puppy Text will provide them with a pet store near them that offers adoptions so they can feel good about where they get their pet supplies.

What will happen during the game?

The Puppy Text is a choose-your-own adventure SMS game where you play the role of a pet shop employee who learns that your store is supporting the inhumane practices of puppy mills. Will you stand up against animal abuse or turn a blind eye to make a profit? At the end of the game, you will receive tips for fighting puppy mills in the real world.

What will you do with my number?

We’ll text you an invitation to start playing the game. We’ll never give out or sell your info.

What will you do with my friends’ numbers?

Your friends will receive an invitation to play the Puppy Text. They can choose to accept or ignore it. We never give out or sell your friends’ info!

How do I win the scholarship?

Send the Puppy Text to six of your friends using the form above and you’ll be entered to win a $3,000 scholarship.

When is the scholarship winner announced?

If you win the scholarship, you will be notified by August 9, 2013. The winner will also be announced on this page.
