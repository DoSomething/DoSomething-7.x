How to Make the Best Card:

Some Tips:

  1. Remember, you don’t know who will be receiving your card, so it’s best to make sure that your card could be enjoyed by anyone.
  2. Make the receiver feel loved by including three fun facts about you in your card: what state you live in, your favorite activity and what you hope to do in the future (i.e. I am from New Jersey, I love to paint, I want to be a doctor).
  3. Be sure that the card is something that you would want to receive on Valentine’s Day!
  4. Remember, these are older readers so make sure you write big and clear.
  5. Get creative! For some card ideas, check out our action guide!
