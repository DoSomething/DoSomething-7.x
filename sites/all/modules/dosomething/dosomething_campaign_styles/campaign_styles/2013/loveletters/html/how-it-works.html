How it works:  

Spread the Love

Depression and isolation affects more than 6.5 million Americans ages 65 and older. Brighten their day by sending them a Valentine’s Day card.

Get Crafty

Send an older adult a handmade Valentine’s card. Include 3 facts about yourself and say Happy Valentine’s Day! Find tips on making the best card here.

Send It

Snap (and upload) a picture of you and your card, find the address of the Meals on Wheels Association of America location of your choice and send the card on its way!
