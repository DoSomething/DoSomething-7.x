How it works:

The Issue

One million children experience homelessness every year. Many kids’ birthdays fall while they are in a shelter, making it hard to celebrate.

Get Crafty

Send a kid at a family shelter a handmade birthday card. It’ll make them feel special. Tips on making the best card are here.

Snail Mail It

Snap (and upload) a picture of you and your card, find the address of the family shelter closest to you here and send the card on its way!
