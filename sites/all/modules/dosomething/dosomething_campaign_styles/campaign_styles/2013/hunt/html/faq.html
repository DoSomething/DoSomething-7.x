Frequently Asked Questions:

What is The Hunt?

The Hunt is an 11 day campaign focused on 11 different causes. The goal of the campaign is to show the world all the awesome ways that teens take action in their community.

Do I have to do all of the challenges?

Nope, you can do as many or as few as you want.

Do I have to do the challenge on the day it’s issued?

No, you can do it whenever you want, as long as you do it by July 26th.

Are you going to use all of the pictures?

We will include as many of the pictures as possible. It depends on how many are submitted, so make them awesome!
