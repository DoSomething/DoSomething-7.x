The Actions

The actions will be released at 10:30am EST daily starting July 16th

What to do

Each day has two actions you can choose on one cause.

The first action is about educating your friends and family on social media.

The second is a real world action that helps your community.

Do one or both! Make sure to take a picture of every action you take and send it to us for your chance to be in our national commercial!

Day 1: Don't be Trashy

Action 1:

Educate your friends by taking a pic of the garbage cans outside your house and sharing it on Instagram with this sentence:

Americans make up only five percent of the world’s population, but we produce almost a quarter of its garbage. #thehunt #environment

Action 2:

Carry your garbage around with you in a bag for the day to show your community how much trash we throw away.

Carry extra bags, so when people ask you what you're doing, tell them the above stat and give them a bag to do the same.

Day 10: Be Prepared

Action 1:

Educate your friends by taking a pic a fire truck and sharing it on Instagram with this sentence:

In 2012, there were 905 natural catastrophes worldwide. #thehunt #disasters

Action 2:

Create a disaster preparedness kit and emergency contact list for a senior citizen in your community &emdash; a neighbor, grandparent, etc. and deliver it to them.

  • Include as many of the following:
  • A list of contacts in the community?
  • Water, one gallon of water per person per day for at least three days, for drinking and sanitation
  • Food, at least a three-day supply of non-perishable (canned) food
  • Battery-powered or hand crank radio and a NOAA Weather, Radio with tone alert and extra batteries for both
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • First aid kit
  • Whistle to signal for help
  • Dust mask to help filter contaminated air and plastic sheeting and duct tape to shelter-in-place
  • Moist towelettes, garbage bags and plastic ties for personal sanitation
  • Wrench or pliers to turn off utilities
  • Manual can opener for food
  • Local maps
  • Cell phone with chargers, inverter or solar charger
all done? show us what you did
