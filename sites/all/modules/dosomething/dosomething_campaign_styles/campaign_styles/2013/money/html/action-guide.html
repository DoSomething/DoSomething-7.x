Tips For Running The Best Drive Ever

Friends are Fun

Running a drive is way easier with friends. Have a friend who’s into art? Put them on flyer duty. That friend you’ve got on student gov? Sounds like a good person to help spread the word.

Time is of the Essence

Find a day and time that works for everyone, or piggyback on events like the big game, assemblies or dances.

Contain Yourself

Location, location, location. Put the box in a high-traffic area (think front of school, local store etc.) or set up multiple can drop-off locations.

Location is Key

Make sure you have somewhere to put all of those empty cans. Check with grocery stores, restaurants, dept. stores to see if they have extra boxes you can snag.

Share the Deets

Here's a stat you can use to get people interested in your drive: When you recycle a can, it saves 95% of the energy it would take to make a brand new one.

Instagram It

Get creative on Instagram! Love art? Make a sculpture with your cans. Feeling competitive? Take a vid of your friends bowling with the cans. We’ll have themes @dosomething #50cans every week for inspiration.
