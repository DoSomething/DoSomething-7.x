Frequently Asked Questions

How many people do I have to have in my workshop?

In order to be entered for the scholarship, you must have at least 2 people attend your workshop. The more the merrier, though! You’ll have a lot more fun if you get together a group of 5 people or more.

I’m leading a session. How do I get entered for a $7,500 scholarship?

You’ll be entered to win the scholarship by visiting this website after hosting your session and selecting “Tell Us About It”. After answering a few brief questions and uploading a picture of your session, you’ll be entered into a drawing for a $7,500 scholarship.

Can I lead more than one session?

Absolutely! You can lead a session for each topic (up to 3), and will get one entry for each submitted session topic’s report back (budgeting, credit, debt). Just submit separate report back forms for each session to get up to three entries for the $7,500 scholarship.

I’m attending a session. How do I get entered for a $2,500 scholarship?

At the end of your session your host will give you a keyword to text to 38383. Answer a few questions via text message to confirm your attendance, and then you’ll be entered in the drawing for a $2,500 scholarship.

Can I attend more than one session?

Definitely. You can attend as many sessions as you would like and are eligible to get one entry in the scholarship drawing per session topic you attend (budgeting, credit, debt).

Where should I run my session?

You can run your sessions anywhere that you can fit a group: A room at school, your community center, church, heck, even your living room would work fine.

How long is each session?

Each session should end up taking about 30 minutes from start to finish and our easy to use handbook will give you everything you need to make it fun!
