Frequently Asked Questions

What's this game about?

Would You Rather was a game where you and your friends will be given crazy, money saving and making situations to choose from. At the end of the game, you were be given a few tips on how you could actually save money a bit easier.

Why should I play?

It's fun, you can see what your friends would do, and you'll all learn something. Awesome.

How do I win the scholarship?

Invite your friends to play with you to be entered to win!

I have a question about Would You Rather? - Who can I email?

Email Farah at WYR@dosomething.org and she'll answer any questions you have.
