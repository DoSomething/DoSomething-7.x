Supermarket Stakeout

Ask the most popular supermarket in town if you can stand outside their entrance on a busy shopping day. Bring your friends, a box, and a poster, and as shoppers go in and out ask them to donate a food item.

Door To Door

Get your neighbors involved! Go door to door and ask your neighbors if they have an extra box, can, or jar of food to help you fight hunger in your community. Make sure to know your hunger facts so you can talk about the importance of donating.

The Classic Food Drive

Follow the steps in our guide to organize a drive at school. If your friends are up for some friendly competition, make it a competition between classes or grades. Make sure to have a cool prize for the winner!
