Don’t know any older adults?

No problem! Older adults at senior centers are always looking to learn new forms of technology.

Find a Senior Center Near You

The easiest way to find a local senior center is to search online for your town + “senior center” or “nursing home.” Usually a phone number/address will pop up.

If you don’t get any results that way, try contacting your town officials or places of worship (like churches or synagogues) to get info. You can also try to find your local senior center through your local Department of Aging.

Don’t Know What to Say When You Call?

Use this script to help you explain what you’d like to do. Having trouble connecting with a senior center? Email Sarah at grandparents@dosomething.org

Hey! My name is _______. I’m a DoSomething.org member and I’m participating in the Grandparents Gone Wired campaign this year.

DoSomething.org is a national non-profit that helps young adults take action on causes they care about, including helping older adults get connected to technology through their Grandparents Gone Wired campaign.

Do you have a volunteer program where I can help older adults get connected to technology? If not, is there a time I can schedule to come in and help people who are interested?

I have resources to help me teach different types of technology. Please let me know if you need me to bring anything else.

Grandparents Gone Wired runs from October 22nd to January 3rd, 2014.

You can reach me at _______ (phone number) or ________ (email).

Thank you!
